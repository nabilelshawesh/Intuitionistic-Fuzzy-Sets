About this test

The GET2 Test is an assessment tool that helps you understand your entrepreneurial tendencies and capabilities. This test contains 54 questions and takes approximately 15 minutes to complete.

Official GET2 Test: For the original GET2 test, please visit www.get2test.net

Our Variation:

This version uses a 5-point Likert scale instead of the original binary (Yes/No) format:
• Strongly Disagree
• Disagree
• Neither Agree nor Disagree
• Agree
• Strongly Agree

This provides more nuanced responses and detailed insights into your entrepreneurial profile.
